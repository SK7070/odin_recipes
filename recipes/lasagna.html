Lasagna Recipes

Description

This is a faster, and less expensive lasagna.

Ingredients

Steps

  1. 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound lean ground beef salt and pepper to taste
  3. 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  4. 1 clove garlic, minced
  5. ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  6. ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  7. 1 pint ricotta cheese
